Specifications

The JBL Live 770NC headphones feature 40mm drivers, Bluetooth 5.3, and a long battery life of up to 65 hours with ANC off. Key specs include True Adaptive Noise Cancelling, voice assistant integration, and a dynamic frequency response range of 20 Hz to 20 kHz wirelessly and 20 Hz to 40 kHz wired. They also have a comfortable, foldable design with a fabric headband, and support multi-point connection and the JBL Headphones App for personalization. Wirelessly stream high-quality sound even at lower bit rates with the help of the latest Bluetooth technology. Audio Mode gets you the best available sound, while Video Mode makes sure the sound and visuals of your movies and games are always in sync.

Features

JBL Signature Sound and surround sound along with powerful JBL Signature Sound, the 40mm drivers offer immersive JBL Spatial Sound that turns any stereo content from any device into virtual surround sound.
True Adaptive Noise Cancelling with Smart Ambient with four noise-sensing mics, means zero distractions, whether it's time to focus on your studies or get your groove on. And if you want to hear the world around you without removing your headphones, Smart Ambient sharpens the sounds of your surroundings.
